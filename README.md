Ever wondered what makes a restaurant tick? 
I dug into real sales data to find out.

## What I Did
Took a messy dataset of 1000+ restaurant orders, 
cleaned it up in Excel and built an interactive 
Power BI dashboard to uncover the story behind the numbers.

## What I Found
- The restaurant made ₹243K in total revenue
- Sandwich is the star — bringing in ₹58K alone
- Night owls spend the most (₹53K after dark!)
- More people pay by Cash (54.57%) than Online

## Tools
Excel · Power BI

- Cleaned data (xlsx)
- Interactive dashboard (pbix)
- Dashboard report (pdf)
